What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are minimally invasive, injectable treatments designed to restore lost volume, smooth out wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. These treatments are perfect for anyone looking to address signs of aging or achieve a more balanced appearance. Common areas treated with dermal fillers include:

  • Lips, to add fullness and definition.
  • Cheeks, to restore volume and lift.
  • Nasolabial folds (smile lines), to smooth deep creases.
  • Under-eye hollows, to reduce tired-looking shadows.
  • Jawline and chin, for improved contour and symmetry.

What to Expect With Dermal Filler in Folsom, CA

Patients will start their process by meeting with Dr. Wallace for a consultation, where we'll get a better understanding of your specific concerns and your long-term aesthetic goals. Additionally, this consultation will be key in helping us determine the most compatible filler or combination of fillers to produce the most favorable outcome.

Benefits of Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are a popular choice for those seeking noticeable results without surgery. Here are some of the key benefits:

  • Immediate Results: Unlike some cosmetic treatments, dermal fillers offer instant improvements, with results continuing to improve over a few days.
  • Customizable Treatment: Each filler session is tailored to your unique facial structure and cosmetic goals.
  • Minimally Invasive: You can quickly return to your daily activities with little to no downtime.
  • Natural-Looking Enhancements: Modern fillers are designed to integrate seamlessly with your natural tissue, ensuring subtle and elegant results.

What's the Difference Between Botox & Fillers?

One of the most common questions patients from across Alabama ask is about the difference between Botox and filler. Both are similar in that they involve using injectable formulas to reverse common aging signs. However, Botox is designed to relax muscles and smooth out wrinkles, and dermal fillers restore lost volume!

Dermal fillers help create a youthful, plump, and firm appearance to the face, smoothing out creases and filling in hollow spaces!
