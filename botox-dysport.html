What is Dysport?

Dysport is an FDA-approved injectable that temporarily improves the appearance of moderate to severe frown lines between the eyebrows, as well as other areas of concern. It works by relaxing targeted facial muscles, softening wrinkles, and helping prevent new lines from forming.

Many clients appreciate Dysport's ability to deliver noticeable yet subtle results. You will still look like yourself—just smoother and more refreshed, without the "frozen" appearance that some people fear.

What Makes Dysport Different from Botox?

Unlike Botox, Dysport uses a more diluted formula. This unique solution allows Dysport to spread throughout the treatment area easily. Dysport works more effectively than Botox in large surface areas. However, you likely need a few more injections than Botox to achieve the same results.

Patients can benefit from Dysport because of its control, flexibility, and effectiveness. Dysport's unique, diluted compound absorbs faster and deeper into targeted muscles. We trust both Dysport and Botox in Tuscaloosa to relax muscles for TMJ treatments, anti-aging, and more!

The Benefits of Dysport

Dysport is known for delivering fast, natural-looking results, but what truly sets it apart is how it works beneath the surface. Its unique formulation and performance characteristics offer distinct advantages compared to other neurotoxin injections.

Some of the key benefits of choosing Dysport include:

  • Faster onset of results, often appearing within 2 to 3 days, compared to the typical 5 to 7 days with other treatments
  • A lightweight formula that disperses smoothly, allowing for effective treatment of larger areas like the forehead with fewer injection points
  • Smoother, more natural movement, helping you maintain facial expressions without stiffness Long-lasting results, with many clients enjoying effects for 3 to 4 months before needing a touch-up
  • Proven track record of safety and effectiveness, backed by extensive clinical research and FDA approval
  • Subtle, refreshed appearance that enhances your features rather than changing them

Dysport's unique diffusion and fast action make it a top choice for a balanced, natural, and effective wrinkle-smoothing solution. At Indian Hills Aesthetics, our skilled providers customize Dysport treatments to match your individual goals, ensuring you leave looking refreshed and confident.

Is Dysport Right for You?

Dysport is a great option for adults looking for a non-surgical way to reduce the appearance of dynamic wrinkles caused by repetitive facial movements. Ideal candidates are generally healthy, have realistic expectations, and are interested in achieving a natural, refreshed appearance.

During your consultation, we will take the time to understand your goals and recommend whether Dysport or another option is the right fit. Our priority is to make sure you feel informed, comfortable, and excited about your next steps.
